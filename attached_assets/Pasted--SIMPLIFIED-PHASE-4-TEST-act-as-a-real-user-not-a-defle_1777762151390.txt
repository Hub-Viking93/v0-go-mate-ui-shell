  WHAT TO VERIFY (per persona, end-to-end)                        
  ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════                                                       
  
  1. EXTRACTION QUALITY:                                                                                                
     - After bootstrap message, side panel should show 6-10 fields
       populated within 5 seconds                                                                                       
     - Field values match what was said (no "I'm 30 years old" stored
       in birth_year — that's the v1 bug, must be fixed)                                                                
     - Audit icons next to each field                                                                                   
                                                                                                                        
  2. QUESTION QUALITY:                                                                                                  
     - Questions are RELEVANT to the persona (Roselle gets sambo-visa-                                                  
       related questions, Axel gets posting-related questions, etc.)                                                    
     - Questions don't repeat anything already extracted                                                                
     - Questions are phrased warmly, not robotic                                                                        
     - When you give an answer, the next question reflects it                                                           
                                                                                                                        
  3. SIDE PANEL SYNC:                                                                                                   
     - When AI is asking question N, side panel shows previously-                                                       
       answered fields and indicates "next: [field]"                                                                    
     - When you answer, side panel updates within 2 seconds
                                                                                                                        
  4. ONBOARDING COMPLETION:                                                                                             
     - After 6-10 substantive answers, AI should say "Profile complete,                                                 
       ready to generate your plan" or similar                                                                          
     - "Generate my plan" button becomes available                
                                                                                                                        
  5. RESEARCH (after clicking Generate):                                                                                
     - Agent panels appear showing the right specialists per persona:                                                   
       * Roselle: visa, tax, cost-of-living, housing, cultural,                                                         
         documents, family-reunion (sambo visa context)                                                                 
       * Axel: ... + posted-worker, school                                                                              
       * Priya: ... no school/pet/family-reunion                                                                        
       * Stevenson: ... + school + pet                                                                                  
     - Each specialist completes within reasonable time (~10-30s)                                                       
     - Specialists scrape REAL official sources (Migrationsverket for                                                   
       Sweden, DGT for Spain, etc.)                                                                                     
     - Citations are real URLs that resolve                                                                             
                                                                                                                        
  6. DASHBOARD QUALITY:                                                                                                 
     - Dashboard renders profile-aware cards (different cards per                                                       
       persona, per the dispatch list above)                                                                            
     - Each card shows REAL DATA from the research                                                                      
     - Roselle's visa card mentions Filipino-specific apostille from                                                    
       DFA Manila                                                                                                       
     - Axel's posted-worker card mentions A1 certificate AND PWD  
     - Priya's tax card mentions US-Spain tax treaty + Beckham Law                                                      
     - Stevenson's pet card mentions Australia's strict quarantine                                                      
       requirements (~10 days minimum)                                                                                  
     - Audit icons on every claim — click to see chain-of-evidence                                                      
                                                                                                                        
  7. DOCUMENT CHECKLIST:                                                                                                
     - Generated checklist is correct for the visa type                                                                 
     - Documents are persona-relevant (Filipino documents for Roselle,                                                  
       German Apostille for Axel, etc.)                                                                                 
     - Each document has a source URL (where to obtain it)

  BUG-HANDLING (binding)                                          
  ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════                                                       
                                                                                                                        
  If anything in the verification list above fails for any persona:                                                     
                                                                                                                        
  1. STOP. Investigate root cause. Read actual code, actual SSE events,                                                 
     actual DB state. Don't guess.                                
                                                                                                                        
  2. FIX at the source. Acceptable fixes:                                                                               
     - Tighten extractor prompt to prevent hallucination
     - Add validation step                                                                                              
     - Fix Question Director's required-fields calculation        
     - Correct dispatch logic in Coordinator                                                                            
     - Fix dashboard's shouldShow predicate                                                                             
     - Tighten specialist's source URL extraction                                                                       
                                                                                                                        
  3. DO NOT acceptable as fixes:                                                                                        
     - Workaround in test code                                                                                          
     - .skip on the failing assertion                                                                                   
     - try/catch to silence the error
     - Comment out the verification                                                                                     
     - Lower a quality threshold to make wrong output "acceptable"
                                                                                                                        
  4. RE-RUN the persona that failed AFTER fix. Must pass cleanly.                                                       
     Then re-run all 4 personas back-to-back. All 4 must pass.                                                          
                                                                                                                        
  5. DOCUMENT each bug + root cause + fix in                                                                            
     artifacts/test-reports/phase-4-bugs-fixed.md.
