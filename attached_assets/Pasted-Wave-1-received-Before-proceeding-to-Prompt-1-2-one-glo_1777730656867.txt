  Reasoning:                                                                                                            
  1. This is a relocation domain app, not a complex reasoning system.
     Sonnet 4.6 handles every agent role here cleanly — coordinator                                                     
     dispatch, specialist research, synthesis, critique. Opus is for                                                    
     problems where Sonnet visibly fails. None of our roles fall there.

     (step 7) where users watch specialists work in real time.                                                          
     Latency-per-token matters more than peak reasoning here. Sonnet                                                    
     produces visibly faster panels — better UX for the buildathon                                                      
     submission video and judges' walkthrough.                                                                          
  3. "Used Opus" isn't a buildathon-scoring lever. "Agents work fast                                                    
     and produce grounded, cited output" is. We pick the model that                                                     
     wins on the visible criteria.
